# Collector Mode

> **Coming soon.** Collector Mode is not yet live. This page describes the planned mechanics.

Collector Mode is the high-stakes layer of guessmyNFT. When you play in Collector Mode, your NFT is on the line.

## How It Works

1. Both players enter the game with one NFT from the collection
2. The game plays out normally — questions, ZK proofs, guesses
3. The loser transfers their NFT to the winner
4. The winner pays a **10% claim fee**

## The Fee

The 10% claim fee is split automatically:

| Destination | Share | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Collection buybacks | 90% | Buy NFTs from the collection — returned to community |
| Protocol treasury | 10% | Fund protocol development |

This creates a flywheel: every Collector Mode game strengthens the collection floor.

## The Rarity Paradox

In Collector Mode, rare NFTs are liabilities. Common NFTs are weapons.

Rare traits are easy to identify — making rare NFTs highly guessable (high GI Score). Common traits are hard to distinguish — making common NFTs the optimal Collector Mode asset.

The game inverts traditional NFT value hierarchies. Floor NFTs become competitive weapons. Trophy assets become risks.

## Guessability Index (GI Score)

The GI Score measures how easily an NFT can be guessed in-game:

| GI Score | Rarity | Collector Mode Strategy |
|---|---|---|
| 85-100 | Ultra-rare (top 5%) | High risk — avoid wagering |
| 60-84 | Rare (top 20%) | Use with caution |
| 35-59 | Uncommon | Moderate risk |
| 10-34 | Common (bottom 50%) | Optimal — hard to guess |
